Key Vision Tasks in Action

  • Lane Keeping: segment drivable area and center the vehicle.
  • Traffic Light/Sign Recognition: detect state and comply.
  • Pedestrian/Cyclist Safety: fast detection + tracking + prediction.
  • Overtaking & Merging: detect vehicles, estimate velocity, plan a gap.
  • Parking: near-field perception with camera + ultrasonic.

Mini Case Studies

Urban Left Turn — crowded intersection

System tracks oncoming vehicles and pedestrians, predicts gaps, and plans a protected/unprotected turn accordingly.

  1. Detect lanes & light state
  2. Track oncoming speeds
  3. Predict crossing pedestrians
  4. Commit when safe margin ≥ threshold
Highway Merge — ramp to mainline

Uses radar for relative velocity and camera for lane geometry, fusing both to accept a safe gap.

Nighttime Pedestrian — low light

Thermal/IR cameras or radar-assisted detection reduce false negatives; vehicle slows proactively.
